Laila Maiden

Laila is a journalist who holds an extensive background in foreign policy and international relations. Her most recent work involved monitoring nuclear weapons negotiations and intergovernmental discussions on the weaponization of space and dual-purpose technology.

She is currently studying at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism where she is both specializing in economics and business reporting and training to cover the upcoming 2020 presidential elections.
